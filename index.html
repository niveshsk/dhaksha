About NEET

National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET) is an All india Entrance Exam to secure admission for MBBS,BDS, BAMS(Ayurveda),BSMS(Siddha),BHMS(Homeopathy),BVSc & AH (Veterinary Science and Animal Husbandry).

Subjects to be Covered (NCERT Syllabus)

Physics

Chemistry

Biology ( Botany & Zoology)

Question Pattern

No.Of Questions 180 ( 45 Question from Each Subject)
Marking scheme 4 marks for each correct answer,
1 Negative Mark for Each Wrong Answer
Maximum marks 720
Type of Question MCQ
Time duration 3 Hrs

NEET Training in Dhaksha

We at Dhaksha offer a crafted teaching methodology to make our young minds quickly grasp the concepts and stand ahead of others. The materials we provide to our children is mentored by Faculty from Top medical colleges around the country along with suggestions from top ranked medical students. The Foundation courses (Grade 11 and Grade 12) and Pre-Foundation Courses (Grade 6 – Grade 10) are designed to train our students from the scratch.


Why we need Pre-foundation Courses?

The questions in NEET exam tests the basics of the core subjects. Students who are strong in basics clear the exam more easier than others. The Continuous learning gives the children gain more confidence to face and clear the exam.

Test series

Test series we provide at Dhaksha is on par with the questions of actual exam and the discussion session after the test makes the students ready to attempt even complex questions.






Methodology

Weekly tests are conducted to evaluate the understanding level of the children. These evaluations are done through the Artificial Intelligence enhanced portal that checks the speed and accuracy of the child. On one weekend result discussion and improvement sessions are scheduled to make the children to keep in track. Dhaksha Academy, one of the best NEET coaching institutes in Tirunelveli carefully crafted the test series to make our children to get improved in Analytical and Problem solving skill which helps them to crack the exam at ease.
